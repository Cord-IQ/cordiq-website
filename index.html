02 — What We Build

Everything your Discord community needs.

From custom bots to complete automation and security systems, every solution is designed around how your community operates.

Custom Discord Bots

Purpose-built bots designed around your community, workflows, and goals.

  • Slash commands and interactive components
  • Ticket and support systems
  • Economy and leveling systems
  • Applications and verification
  • Community engagement tools
  • Fully custom functionality

Automation & Workflows

Reduce manual work with connected systems that run reliably in the background.

  • Welcome and onboarding flows
  • Autoroles and self-role panels
  • Scheduled messages and events
  • Alerts and notifications
  • Temporary voice channels
  • Custom automated processes

Moderation & Security

Protect your server with dependable moderation tools and automated threat response.

  • Anti-raid and anti-nuke protection
  • Spam and bad-word filtering
  • Warning and punishment systems
  • Audit logs and staff controls
  • Captcha and verification
  • Custom security rules

Integrations & Support

Connect Discord with external platforms and keep the complete system maintained.

  • API and database integrations
  • Twitch and Kick alerts
  • External verification services
  • Application and payment systems
  • Maintenance and upgrades
  • Ongoing technical support
